This article, it poses some moral questions about technology that we should consider when innovating new technologies. I found these questions interesting as it brings a whole new perspective to how we view tech these days. I feel as though the focus on it right now is automating things and utilizing it to make efficient and money-saving moves. But these questions force you to take a step back and consider other implications that technology can bring upon us. Although all these questions have a purpose in their own ways individually, I believe that it can be cumbersome to consider every single one. I think taking into account the general vicinity of what the questions are trying to capture is sufficient in raising the morality of tech. So, even though I do think these questions are critical, it does seem a little overboard to have to answer all of them. Overall, I couldn’t agree more with the fact that there is a moral aspect to technology that we often forget, and it is being more and more important that we need to acknowledge it to a certain extent.
