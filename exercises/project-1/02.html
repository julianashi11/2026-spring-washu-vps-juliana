Songbird

Supporting local isn’t just a philosophy—it’s at the heart of how we cook, connect, and serve.

At Songbird, we believe great mornings start with great ingredients. That’s why our menus sing with the best of every season—featuring fresh, locally-sourced produce, responsibly raised meats, and small-batch goods from nearby farms and purveyors who share our commitment to sustainability. We proudly serve Blueprint Coffee, and celebrate the rich flavors of our region with every plate.
