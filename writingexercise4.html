Something that I long for is sitting down at the dinner table and just eating with my family. Back in my childhood, it was everyday where my family and I would eat dinner together and talk about our day. Back then, it was the norm. I would come home, do some homework, and sit down to eat a delicious meal. I now miss it so much. A few months ago, it felt so ordinary, but now that I am in college, I yearn for the same comfort from my family. Even days where we would sit in silence, slurping up the delicious food, are some of the moments that I crave. I realize now that my childhood is slowly starting to be behind me, and now I must grow in my own shoes. I was always the one getting taken care of, but now I must take care of myself; later, I will have to take care of my family. These little things, from the nightly dinners with my family to grabbing pizza with my grandma, are moments that I miss so dearly. Just the warmth of my family is enough to keep me at peace, and not having them with me in college has been rough, but it has taught me so much about independence. I still give my parents a call every day, and that is what makes me so grateful about technology. The fact that we can stay in contact thousands of miles away is a blessing. Now that school is almost ending, and I can go back home, I will make sure to cherish those little moments and spend more time soaking up all the happy laughs.
