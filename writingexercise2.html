A human-made creation that brings out the best in me is chopsticks. From a young age, chopsticks have been a huge part of my life. Whether that is struggling to get the hang of it at the age of 4 or devouring the most delicious foods with it, I would say that it has impacted more to my life that one would expect. Without chopsticks, food truly tastes different. The woody and soft feeling of the utensil elevates the flavors of food, especially Asian cuisine. Though it might have been extremely hard for me to get used to using chopsticks, even though I still hold it incorrectly, it has bought so much joy and comfort into my life in indirect ways. It has taught me that struggle and perseverance always comes with a reward at the end. Not even just the fact that it allows me to eat delicious food, but it has the ability to bring people together. From setting the dinner table to creating my own custom chopsticks, I have found ways that doing these things brings the people I love together. So, even though chopsticks might be a mundane thing in most people’s lives, I believe that this creation is one that has changed my life in more ways than I would have expected. Furthermore, looking into the future, I am excited to see different ways how to think about using chopsticks symbolically.
