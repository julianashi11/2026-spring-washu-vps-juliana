The documentary was based on Eno, a record producer who utilizes technology to create ambient music. I personally really enjoyed being able to watch the process of creating music. You can tell the music he produces is very intentional and that he bases his music choices on his feelings and knowledge. He describes “feelings as the beginning of thoughts.” What I truly enjoyed about this documentary was his passion for building out music incrementally. Almost replicating the process of creating a painting. Eno truly revolutionized the music that you could produce in the studio, which provoked unconventional methods of creating contemporary music. I think this documentary connects to this class as it creates a website that can evoke the same feeling that music can. Just like music, a website can also have a certain vibe that makes the user truly feel like they are almost inside the screen. Moving on to the next project, my goal is to emulate that feeling and create a space within the screen. Inspired by his music, I might also add some audio aspects to the website as well!
